[The Floods and Droughts Research Infrastructure (FDRI)](https://fdri.org.uk/) is a £38 million UK research infrastructure programme designed to improve understanding, prediction and management of floods and droughts across the UK. It is funded by the Natural Environment Research Council and coordinated by UK Centre for Ecology & Hydrology (UKCEH), working with Imperial College London, University of Bristol and British Geological Survey.
